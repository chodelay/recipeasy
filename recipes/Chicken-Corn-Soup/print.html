Chicken Corn Soup

Ingredients

6 cupsChicken Stock
1Chicken Breast Fillet
300 gCanned Corn Kernels
300 gCanned Creamed Corn
1 tbspCornflour
2 tbspLight Soy Sauce
2Eggs
2Spring Onionssliced

Utensils

1 tsbpMeasure
3Small Prep Bowl
1Large Pot
1Fork

Steps

1. Prep

5 min
  • Slice spring onions and put aside in small prep bowl.
  • Beat eggs and put aside in small prep bowl.
  • Combine soy sauce and cornflour into a paste and set aside in small prep bowl.

2. Poach Chicken

5 min
20 min
  • Prepare stock in pyrex jug.
  • Bring the stock to the boil in a large saucepan.
  • Add chicken breast fillet to the stock, turn the heat off and cover the pan with a lid for 15 minutes.
  • Remove chicken breast from the stock and leave to cool for a few minutes then shred.

3. Cook Soup

10 min
  • Add corn to stock and bring to the boil over a medium heat.
  • Add soy sauce / corn flour mix and stir in.
  • Add shredded chicken to soup.
  • Slowly pour beaten eggs into the soup in a steady stream, stirring constantly with a fork.
