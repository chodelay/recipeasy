Teriyaki Don

Ingredients

Teriyaki Marinade

1 cupSoy Sauce
1/2 cupMirin
1/4 cupWhite Vinegarpref. rice wine
1/2 tbspGingerdiced finely
2 tbspGarlicdiced finely
2 tbspHoney
1 tbspScriracha
Salt
Pepper
Red Chili Flakes
1 kgChickenboneless

Marinade

Cook

Steps

1. Prep & Marinade

15 min
2 hours
  • Dice/mince ginger and garlic.
  • Trim chicken and slice into small pieces.
  • Measure out marinade ingredients into bowl and mix in chicken.
  • Leave mariande for at least 2 hrs.

2. Wok Fry

5 min
  • Heat oil in a wok til smoking.
  • Add chicken, avoiding too much marinade.
  • Cook through tossing to keep sauce moving.
