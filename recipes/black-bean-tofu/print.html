Black Bean Tofu

Ingredients

420 gFirm Tofu
3 tbsp oilDivided
2 clovesGarlicminced
2 tbspDried Fermented Black Beansrinsed
2Scallionslarge pieces, whites and greens separated
2Dried Red Chiliesdeseeded and chopped (optional)
1 tbspShaoxing Wine
½ tbspLight Soy Sauce
½ tspSesame Oil
¼ tspGround White Pepper
¼ tspSugar
1 tspCornstarchdissolved in 2 tbsp water

Utensils

1Prep Bowl (Medium)
1Wok
1Egg Flip

Steps

1. Prep

2 min
  • Cut the tofu into quarter inch thick squares.
  • Pat each piece of tofu dry with a paper towel, and set aside in medium prep bowl

2. Fry Tofu

3 min
  • Place a clean wok or cast iron skillet over high heat until it just starts to smoke.
  • Turn the heat down to medium, and add 2 tbsp of oil to the wok.
  • Fry the tofu on both sides until lightly golden brown.

3. Cook Flavour

Add 1 tbsp of oil to wok of medium heat.
  • Add garlic, black beans, white parts of the scallions, andStir and cook everything for a minute, taking care not to burn the garlic. Add in the tofu, the Shaoxing wine, light soy sauce, sesame oil, white pepper, sugar, and the green parts of the scallions. chopped chilies.
  • Stir and cook everything for a minute, taking care not to burn the garlic.
  • Add the tofu, Shaoxing wine, light soy sauce, sesame oil, white pepper, sugar, and the green parts of the scallions.
  • Stir-fry gently so as to not break up the tofu. When the mixture is bubbling.
  • Stir the cornstarch until completely dissolved.
  • Then add it to the wok, stirring gently and quickly until the sauce has thickened and evenly coats the tofu. Serve immediately!
