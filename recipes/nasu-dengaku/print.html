Nasu Dengaku

Ingredients

2Eggplant
2 tbspVegetable Or Canola Oil
1/4 cupMiso Paste
2 tbspMirin
1 tbspGranulated Sugar
1 tbspSake
Sesame Seeds

Utensils

Knife
Chopping Board
Pan (With Lid)
Brush

Steps

1. Prep

5 min
  • Preheat oven to 280C
  • Slice eggplant in half and score the inside in small squares.
  • Mix miso, mirin, sugar and sake in a medium prep bowl.

2. Pan

5 min
10 min
  • Heat oil in a pan over high heat and put the eggplant facing skin-down.
  • Cook for a few minutes until skin is brown. Turn the eggplant over and cover with a lid.
  • Cook until eggplant is cooked through (about 3 to 4 minutes).

3. Oven

2 min
6 min
  • Cover a cooking tray with foil and place the eggplant on top.
  • Brush miso dengaku mix on top of each eggplant until all the surface is coated.
  • Put under the grill for 4 minutes. The miso mix should be bubbling when you take it out.
  • Sprinkle sesame seeds on top and serve hot.
