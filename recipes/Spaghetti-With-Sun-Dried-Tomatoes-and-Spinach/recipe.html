Spaghetti With Sun Dried Tomatoes And Spinach

Ingredients

400gSpaghetti
1/4 cupExtra-Virgin Olive Oil
1 cloveGarlicthinly sliced lengthwise
1 cupSundried Tomatoeschopped
5 cupsWashed Spinach Leaves
2 tbspBalsamic Vinegar
1 cupCrumbled Feta Cheese
to tasteSalt And Black Pepper

Utensils

Steps

1.

20 min
  • Cut sundried tomatoes and put aside with balsamic vinegar and spinach.
  • Put spaghetti on.
  • In a large skillet, heat 1 tbsp of oil.
  • Add sun dried tomatoes, spinach, and balsamic vinegar.
  • Cook over medium-high heat until spinach is wilted, about 3-4 minutes.
  • Toss spaghetti with the sun dried tomatoes and spinach.
  • Stir in the 1/2 cup pasta water.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Sprinkle spaghetti with feta cheese and toasted garlic chips. Serve warm.
