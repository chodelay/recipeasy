Mushroom Tofu And Vermicelli Soup

Ingredients

4 cupsChicken Stock
2 tbspSoy Sauce
1 tbspMiso Paste
1 tbspMirin
1 tbspSake
120gTofucut into 1/2 inch cubes
90gShiitake Mushroomsthinly sliced, stems discarded
60gEnoki Mushrooms
60gVermicelli Noodles
3Shallotssliced diagonally

Utensils

Steps

1. Prep

5 min
  • Slice mushrooms and green onions.

2. Cook Soup

10 min
  • Add the chicken stock, soy sauce, miso paste, mirin and sake to a medium saucepan and bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat and simmer for 2 minutes.
  • Add the tofu, mushrooms, and vermicelli, return to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 6 minutes.
  • Add the green onions and simmer for another 2 minutes.
