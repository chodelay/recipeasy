Steps

1. Marinade Prep

10 min
  • ChopÂ 10 garlic cloves and put 8 cloves worth aside in small prep bowl
  • Chop or grate 3 tbsp of ginger
  • Slice beef into medium sized cubes

2. Marinate Meat

5 min
30 min
  • Add into bowl: 2 cloves Garlic, Ginger, 5 tbsp Fish Sauce, 2.5 tsp Five Spice Powder, 1.5 tsp Brown Sugar
  • Mix and coat beef

3. Lemongrass

5 min
  • Remove woody parts from lemongrass
  • Cut 1 stalk into 3 inch lengths, put in bowl
  • Mince the other 2 stalks and put in garlic bowl

4. Onions

5 min
  • Slice the onion(s) into thin wedges and place in medium prep bowl
  • (When it comes to cooking, reserve some for garnish)

5. Stock

5 min
  • Grind 1 tsp of annatto seeds
  • Mix in Pyrex Jug: 8 cups water, 2 cups coconut water, 2 star anise, 1 tsp ground black pepper, 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp annatto seeds and 1 tbsp paprika

6. Stew A

10 min
1 hour.
  • Heat 3 tbsp of oil over high heat.
  • Add lemongrass stalks for 1 min to infuse.
  • Add minced lemongrass, garlic, onions (not all). Cook through.
  • Add beef to the pot & brown evenly.
  • Add 4 tbsp tomato paste. Stir & cook for 5 min.
  • Add pyrex jug of stock. Bring to boil - reduce to medium low and simmer for an hour.

7. Carrots

5 min
  • Peel carrots
  • Slice into 1.5 inch chunks with diagonal edges

8. Sauces

5 min
  • Into small bowl:
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 3 tsp of chili oil

9. Stew B

1 min
40 min
  • Add carrots, salt, soy sauce & chili oil.
  • Simmer for another 40 min.

10. Noodles & Serve

5 min
  • Remove anise pods & lemongrass stalks.
  • Cook noodles
  • Noodles into bowl, soup on top
