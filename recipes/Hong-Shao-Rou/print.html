Hong Shao Rou

Ingredients

Pork & Marinade

700gPork Bellycut into 3/4-inch thick pieces
4 tbspOil
2 tbspSugarpreferably rock
6 tbspShaoxing Wine
2 tbspLight Soy Sauce
1 tbspDark Soy Sauce
4 cupsWater
1Star Anise
1 tspChinese Five Spice

Utensils

1Bowl, Large
1Chopping Board

Steps

1. Prep Pork

10 min
  • Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  • Cut your pork into 3/4 inch thick pieces and put into large prep bowl.
  • Blanch the pork for 3 minutes.
  • Take the pork out of the pot and set aside.

2. Cook In Sauce

15 min
1 hour
  • Over low heat, add oil and sugar to your wok.
  • Melt the sugar slightly and add the pork.
  • Raise the heat to medium and cook until the pork is lightly browned.
  • Turn the heat back down to low and add Shaoxing cooking wine, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and water.
  • Cover and simmer for up to an hour until pork is fork tender.
  • Every 5-10 minutes, stir to prevent burning and add more water if it gets too dry.
  • If there is still a lot of visible liquid, uncover the wok, turn up the heat, and stir continuously until the sauce has reduced to a glistening coating.
