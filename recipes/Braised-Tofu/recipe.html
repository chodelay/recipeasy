Braised Tofu

Ingredients

400gFirm Tofu, Each Cut Into 16 Pieces
4 tbspOil
3Garlic Clovesminced
1Red Chilithinly sliced
1 1/2 tbspFish Sauce
1 1/2 tablespoonSoy Sauce
1/2 tablespoonShaoxing Wine
1/2 tablespoonsSugar
1/8 teaspoonGround White Pepper
1/2 cupWater
2Scallionscut into 1-inch pieces

Utensils

Steps

1.

20min
  • Heat oil in a frying pan and fry the tofu pieces until golden brown. Set aside.
  • Saut√© garlic and chilis until fragrant, about 3 minutes. Then add vegan fish sauce, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, Shaoxing, sugar, ground white pepper, and water. Mix well and bring to a boil.
  • Return the tofu to the pan, reduce heat, and simmer until the sauce is reduced by half, about 10 minutes.
  • Add the scallions, mix well, and cook for another minute. Turn off heat, transfer to a serving plate and serve with steamed white rice.
