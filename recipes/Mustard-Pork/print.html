Mustard Pork

Ingredients

Sauce

4Pork Fillets
1 1/2 tspCurry Powder
30 gButter
1 tbspLemon Juice

Pork

1 cupWater
1Stock Cubechicken
2 tspLemon Juice
2 tspFrench Mustard
2 tbspCream
2 tspCornflour
2 tspWater

Seasoning

Salt
Pepper

Utensils

Measures

1 cup
1 tsp
1 tbsp

Other

Pyrex Jug
Pot
Saucepan

Steps

1. Prep

10 min
  • Trim all fat from pork and slice into pieces.
  • Sprinkle curry powder over pork pieces and rub in.

2. Sauce

10 min
  • Put water, crumbled stock cube, lemon juice, french mustard and cream in saucepan.
  • Combine cornflour and water and stir into saucepan.
  • Stir until sauce boils and thickens.
  • Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, 3 minutes.
  • Season with salt and pepper.

3. Pork

20 min
  • Melt butter in a baking dish, add fillets, pour lemon juice over.
  • Bake, uncovered, in moderate oven 15 minutes or until tender.
  • Baste meat frequently during cooking.
  • Remove fillets, slice thinly and serve with mustard sauce.
