Chicken Schnitzel

Ingredients

Chicken

2Chicken Breast Fillets
160gFresh Breadcrumbs
1/3 cupFinely Grated Parmesan
1LemonZested and wedged
1/4 cupFresh Parsleyfinely chopped
1Egg
1/2 cupMilk
Plain Flourto dust
Olive Oilto shallow-fry

Tomatosauce

1 tspOlive Oil
1Brown Onionchopped
4Garlic Cloveminced
400 gDiced Tomatoes canned
1 tspSugar
1/4 cup Basil shredded
12Olivesoptional

Topping

3-4 stripsBaconmiddle
1 cupCheesegrated
1/2 cupParmesangrated

Utensils

1Zester
3Prep Bowl, Medium
3Prep Bowl, Small
1Large Baking Tray
1Shallow Ovenproof Dish
Baking Paper
Tongs
Knife
Mallet
Chopping Board

Steps

1. Assembly Line

5min
  • Preheat oven to 180C.
  • In a medium bowl, mix breadcrumbs with parmesan, lemon zest, parsley, and ground black pepper.
  • In another medium bowl, whisk together the egg and milk.
  • Dust a plate with flour.
  • Line a large baking tray with baking paper.

2. Prep Chicken

5min
30min
  • Trim breasts and slice into two fillets each.
  • Wrap the breasts in plastic and flatten with mallet.
  • Cover chicken in flour, then egg wash, then breadcrumbs. Place on baking tray.
  • Set aside.

3. Prep Sauce

5min
  • Chop onion into fine pieces and put in medium prep bowl.
  • Mince garlic into same prep bowl.
  • Chop basil (opt: and olives) into small prep bowl.

4. Cook Sauce

10min
30min
  • Heat oil in small saucepan on medium heat.
  • Add onion and garlic and cooked uncovered until onion soft (6-7min), stirring occasionally.
  • Add tomatoes and simmer until thick (15-20 min).
  • Start the next step.

5. Cook Schnitzels

10min
  • Spray the chicken with cooking spray.
  • Bake for 10 minutes.
  • Preheat the grill to medium towards the end of this.
  • (opt: place bacon on here to ensure warmth later)

6. Grill Schnitzels

5-10min
  • Transfer chicken to shallow dish and place bacon across it.
  • Stir sugar, basil (opt: and olives) into tomato sauce.
  • Spoon sauce over chicken.
  • Top with cheese.
  • Grill for 5 minutes or until golden.
