Pork Black Bean Bean Stir Fry

Ingredients

Sauce

500 gGround Pork
2 tbspSoy Sauce
2 tspRice Wine
¼ tspPeppercorns (Szechuan)freshly ground
¼ cupChicken Stock
1 tspSpicy Garlic Black Bean Sauce
1 tspHoisin Sauce
1-1/2 tspGarlic Chili Sauce
1-1/2 tspSugar
1 tspSesame Oil
2 tspCornstarch

Stir Fry

¼ cupVegetable Oil
500 gFresh Green Beans trimmed and cut in half if desired
3Garlic Clovesminced
1 tspFresh Gingerpeeled and minced
4Green Onionssliced
¼ cupWhite Sesame Seeds, Lightly Toasted

Utensils

1 tsp
¼ tsp
1 tbsp
2Bowls (Medium)
2Bowls (Small)

Steps

1. Marinade & Sauce

5 min
  • Ground peppercorns.
  • In a medium prep bowl toss the pork with 1 tablespoon of soy sauce, 1 teaspoon of rice wine and the ground pepper.
  • Marinate at room temperature while assembling the rest of the ingredients.
  • Combine 1 tbsp of soy sauce, 1 tsp of rice wine, chicken stock, spicy bean sauce, hoisin, chili garlic sauce, sugar, sesame oil and cornstarch in a small prep bowl.
  • Cut the garlic and into fine pieces and put aside with the pork.
  • Slice the green onion, put a quarter aside for garnish and the rest for frying.

2. Beans

10 min
  • Heat oil in a wok or large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  • Add the beans and stir fry them for 10 minutes, or until they begin to brown and blister.
  • Remove the beans from the pan using a slotted spoon and pour off all but 2 tablespoons of the oil.

3. Stir Fry

5 min
  • Return the pan to the heat and add the pork, garlic and ginger.
  • Stir fry for about 3 minutes, or until there is no longer any pink color to the pork.
  • Add the green onions and cook for 1 minute.
  • Add the reserved sauce and green beans and mix well.
  • Cook until sauce thickens and glazes the pork and beans.
  • Take pan off heat and toast sesame seeds.
  • Serve on rice or pan fried noodles, garnishing with sliced green onions and sesame seeds.
