Burger Patties

Ingredients

800 gGround Beef
1Eggbeaten
2 slicesBreadplain
6 tbspEvaporated Milk
2 tbspWorcestershire Sauce
1/8 tspCayenne Pepper
2 clovesGarlicminced

Utensils

Bowls

2Prep Bowl (Large)
1Prep Bowl (Small)

Measures

1 tbsp
1/8 tbsp

Tools

1Knife
1-2Fork/Whisk
1Chopping Board

Steps

1. Prep

3 min
  • Chop the garlic.
  • Beat the egg in a small prep bowl.

2. Panade

5 min
  • In a large prep bowl, make a panade as follows:
  • Add the evaporated milk to the bread slices, pressing on bread to allow milk to absorb.
  • Pull apart and mix bread with fork, making a paste.

3. Pattie Mixture

2 min
  • In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, egg, panade, Worcestershire sauce, cayenne pepper, and garlic.
  • Form the mixture into patties.

4. Cook Patties

5 min
  • Heat oil in pan and add patties.
  • Flip patties often and evenly for 4 minutes total.
