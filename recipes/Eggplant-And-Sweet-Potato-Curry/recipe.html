Eggplant And Sweet Potato Curry

Ingredients

2 tbspVegetable Oil
400gDiced Tomatoes
400mlCoconut Cream
1 tbspBrown Sugar
1/2 tspSalt
1/2 cupBasil Leaves

Flavour Base

1Red Onioncut into wedges
3 cloves Garliccrushed
1/4 cupThai Red Curry Paste

Filling

1/2Sweet Potatopeeled and cut in a 2cm dice (about 2 cups)
1Eggplantpeeled and cut in to 2cm cubes (about 4 cups)

Utensils

Steps

1. Prep

10 min
  • Cut onion into wedges
  • Peel & crush garlic
  • Peel sweet potato and cut into 2cm cubes and put aside in a large bowl.
  • (Peel eggplant) and cut into 2cm cubes and put aside in same bowl.

2. Cook Vegetables

10 min
  • Place oil in a wok over medium heat, and stir fry the onion and garlic until softened.
  • Add the curry paste and stir for another minute, until fragrant.
  • Add the sweet potato and eggplant and toss to coat in the curry paste.

3. Cook Curry

30 min
  • Add the tomatoes, coconut milk, brown sugar and salt.
  • Bring to the boil and reduce the heat.
  • Leave on a high simmer, stirring occasionally, until the sweet potato is tender and all the vegetables have cooked through.
  • Taste and add more salt or sugar if required.Remove from the heat and stir through basil leaves.Serve with jasmine rice.
