Kentucky Baked Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients

Chicken

8Chicken Drumsticks
2Eggslightly beaten
Canola Oil Spray
Oven Fries And Salad To Serve
Lemonto serve

Herb Mix

1 cupPlain Flour
1/4 tspPepper
1 tspCurry Powder
1/2 tspNutmeg
1/4 tspCayenne Pepper
1/2 tspPaprika
1 1/2 tspDried Parsley
1/2 tspGarlic Powder
1/4 tspSalt

Utensils

1Knife
tspMeasure
1Baking Tray
Baking Paper
1Large Prep Bowl
2Medium Prep Bowl

Steps

1. Prep Chicken

10 min
  • Preheat oven to 220C/ 200C fan-forced.
  • Line the baking tray.
  • Beat eggs & 2 tbsp water into medium prep bowl.
  • Combine flour, spices, salt and parsely in another medium prep bowl
  • Using a sharp knife, make shallow cuts through chicken skin on each drumstick.
  • One at a time, dip drumsticks in egg, then flow mix to coat - shaking off excess.
  • Place drumsticks on baking tray.

2. Cook Chicken

40 min
  • Bake for 15 minutes.
  • Turn chicken.
  • Bake for a further 20 to 25 minutes or until golden and crisp.
  • Serve with salad and lemon.
