Bacon Carbonara

Ingredients

Sauce

2Eggs
1/2 cupCreampref. Double Cream
2/3 cupParmesan
ParsleyFresh or Dry
1/4 tspSalt+ some for pasta
1/2 tspRed Pepper Flakes

Remainder

500 gBacon
3Large Mushroomschopped
1Small Onionchopped
500 gPastaof choice

Utensils

1/4Tsp
1/2Cup
1/3Cup
Whisk
Pot
Pan
Tongs/Flip
Strainer

Steps

1. Prep

5 min
  • Beat the eggs, cream, Parmesan, parsley, salt and the red pepper together in a medium prep bowl.
  • Dice onion finely.
  • Slice mushrooms thinly.
  • Boil water for pasta.

2. Bacon & Pasta

10min
  • Put pasta on.
  • Fry the bacon until crisp, then crumble and set aside.
  • With remaining bacon grease, saute onion and mushrooms until onions are golden. Lower heat if waiting for pasta.
  • Drain the pasta when cooked and while still warm, add to the onions and mushrooms.
  • Pour cream mixture into the pasta, and stir over low heat for 1-2 minutes.
  • Serve with additional Parmesan.
