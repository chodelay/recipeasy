Steps

1. Prep

10 min
  • (Put on rice)
  • Finely slice onions, garlic and capsicum and put into a medium prep bowl
  • In another prep bowl, put diced tomatoes, tomato paste, coconut sugar, sea salt, paprika, cumin, chili powder, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, cardamom, and coriander.
  • Slice olives and drain chick peas and put aside in prep bowl.
  • Chop coriander and put aside.

2. Saute Onions

2 min
5 min
  • Heat a large rimmed metal or cast iron skillet over medium heat.
  • Once hot, add olive oil, onion, bell pepper and garlic.
  • Saut√© for 4-5 minutes, stirring frequently, until soft and fragrant.

3. Cook Sauce

5 min
25 min
  • Add tomato puree or diced tomatoes, tomato paste, coconut sugar, sea salt, paprika, cumin, chili powder, cinnamon, cayenne pepper (optional), cardamom, and coriander (optional).
  • Stir to combine.
  • Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  • Add chickpeas and olives and stir to combine.
  • Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 15-20 minutes.

4. Serve

1 min
  • Add more cumin or paprika for smokiness, cayenne for heat, maple syrup for sweetness, cardamom and coriander for earthiness, or chili powder for smoke/heat.
  • Serve as is or with bread, pasta, or rice.
  • Garnish with lemon juice, olives, coriander/parsley.
