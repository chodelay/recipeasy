Beef Tacos

Ingredients

1 tbspOlive Oil
1Brown Onionfinely chopped
2 tspGround Cumin
2 tspGround Coriander
1 tspPaprika
1/4 tspCayenne Pepper
500 gBeef Mince
400 gCan Crushed Tomatoes
taco shellsJumbo
Iceberg Lettuceshredded
2Tomatoesmedium chopped
1 1/2 cupsGrated Tasty Cheese
1Tomatofresh

1Avocadosliced
1 tubSour Cream

Utensils

Chopping Board
Knife
Scissors
Wooden Spoon
3Prep Bowl - Small
1Prep Bowl - Medium

Serving Implements
Pan

Steps

1. Prep

15 min
  • Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  • Slice onion into medium prep bowl.
  • Slice tomato into small pieces and put aside in serving bowl.
  • Slice avocado into strips and put aside in small prep bowl.
  • Place taco shells on tray.
  • Grate cheese into small prep bowl.
  • Measure Cumin, Coriander and Paprika into small prep bowl.

2. Cook Meat

12 min
  • Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  • Add onion. Cook, stirring, for 3 minutes or until softened.
  • Add cumin, coriander, paprika and cayenne pepper.
  • Cook, stirring, for 30 seconds or until fragrant.
  • Add mince and cook, stirring for 8 minutes or until browned. Start Step 3 during this.

3. Shells

10 min
  • Place taco shells on tray and put in oven for 6-8 minutes.
  • Add crushed tomato. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes or until sauce has thickened.
  • Serve.
