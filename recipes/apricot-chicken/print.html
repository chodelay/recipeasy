Apricot Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken

1/2 cupPlain Flour
SprinkleSalt And Pepper
1 kgSkinless Chicken Thigh Cutlets / Drumsticks

Flavour Base

1Large Brown Onion
8Garlic Cloves
1 tbspCumin
1 tbspCoriander

Stock

4 tbspBrandy
405 mlApricot Nectar
40 gFrench Onion Soup
1/2 cupDried Apricots

To Serve

1 cupRice
1.5 cupWater
1/3 cupFresh Parsley

Utensils

Measures

1/2 cup
1/3 cup
1 tbsp

Landing

2Prep Bowl (Medium)
2Prep Bowl (Small)
2Plates (For Chicken)
Paper Towel
1/personPlate

Tools

1Rice Cooker
1-2Chopping Boards
1Frying Pan (Large W/ Lid)
1Wooden Spoon
1Tongs
1Knife (Veg Then Meat)
1Peeler

Steps

1. Prep

15 min
  • Rinse rice and put in rice cooker with water. Turn on.
  • Boil water and prepare 1/2 a cup of french onion soup in pyrex jug.
  • Cut onion into thin wedges and place in medium prep bowl.
  • Finely chop garlic cloves and place in aforementioned bowl.
  • Add coriander and cumin to aforementioned bowl.
  • Cut parsley and put aside in small prep bowl.
  • Chop apricots into smaller pieces and put aside in small prep bowl.

2. Seal Chicken

10 min
  • Flour, salt, pepper into mixing bowl.
  • Toss chicken in mix, shake off excess.
  • Heat 1 tbsp oil into frying pan on medium heat.
  • Cook chicken in frying pan in batches for 2-3 min each side / golden.
  • Add extra oil as required.
  • Set aside on plate.

3. Sauce

10 min
  • Heat remaining oil in pan.
  • Cook onion, garlic and french onion soup into pan 5 min or until tender, stirring occasionally
  • Add nectar, brandy and french onion soup to pan and stir.
  • Bring to boil then back to low heat.

4. Cook Chicken

20 min
  • Return chicken to pan.
  • Cover & cook for 20 min.

5. Final Touches

25 min
  • Push apricots into the pan.
  • Cook uncovered for 20 to 25 min or until chicken cooked & sauce thick.

6. Serve

1 min
  • Portion out rice.
  • Place chicken on rice.
  • Add parsley and serve.
