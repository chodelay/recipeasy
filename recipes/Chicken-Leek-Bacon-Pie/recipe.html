Steps

1. Prep

15 min
  • Boil water for stock.
  • Chop parsley and put aside in small prep bowl. [A]
  • Chop the leek and crush garlic. Put aside together. [B]
  • Slice the bacon and put aside with the above. [B]
  • Cube the chicken and put aside in a bowl. [C]
  • Prepare stock. [D]
  • Measure 1/3 cup of flour. [E]
  • (Wash the knife and chopping board, put away herbs & flour.)

2. Cook Filling

25 min
1 hour
  • Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  • Cook chicken in batches, turning occasionally, for 2-3 minutes or until browned. [C]
  • Transfer to a plate. Place thyme with chicken on plate. [F]
  • Add the bacon, leek and garlic to the pan on medium-high, stirring, for 5 minutes. [B]
  • Add the flour and cook, stirring, for 30 seconds. Remove from heat. [E]
  • Gradually stir in the stock. [D]
  • Add the chicken and thyme to the pan. [F]
  • Return to medium heat, stirring occasionally, for 8 minutes or until mixture thickens and comes to boil.
  • Season with pepper and set aside for 1/ to cool slightly.
  • (Wash prep bowls, plate for chicken and pyrex jug.)

3. Cool Filling

1 hour
  • Place filling in large bowl, add parsley and put in fridge for .
  • (Wash pan)

4. Cook Pie

40 min
30 min
  • Preheat oven to 220Â°C.
  • Whisk egg in small bowl. [H]
  • Spray pan dish with oil and line with puff pastry.
  • Fill pie with chicken mixture. [G]
  • Brush edge of the dish with egg.
  • Cover pie with puff pastry and push into the base pastry.
  • Brush the pie with the rest of the egg.
  • Add breathing holes / decorations.
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes.
