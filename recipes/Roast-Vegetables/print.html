Roast Vegetables

Ingredients

1 tbspFresh Thymechopped
2 tbspFresh Rosemarychopped
1/4 cupOlive Oil
2 tbspBalsamic Vinegar

Utensils

Steps

1.

10 min
  • Preheat oven to 245C.
  • Slice vegetables into large even pieces.
  • Break onions apart.
  • Chop rosemary and thyme.
  • Combine thyme, rosemary, olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper in large bowl.
  • Toss vegetables in until they are coated.
  • Spread evenly on a large roasting pan.
  • Roast for 35 to 40 minutes in the preheated oven, stirring every 10 minutes, or until vegetables are cooked through and browned.
