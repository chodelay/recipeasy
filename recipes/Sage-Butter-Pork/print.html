Sage Butter Pork

Ingredients

Meat & Marinade

2 tbspDried Sagecrumbled
1 1/2 teaspoonsBrown Sugar
1 1/2 teaspoonsCoarse Salt
1 tspGarlic Powder
1 tspFreshly Ground Black Pepper
850gPork Tenderloins

Sage Butter

6 tbspButterpreferably unsalted
Olive Oil Spray
2 tbspOlive Oil
1/4 cupSage Leaves, Fresh Sprigs

Garnish

1Garlic Cloveslivered
1/4 cupSage Leaves, Fresh Sprigs

Utensils

Chopping Board
Knife
Foil
Griddle Pan (With Cover)
Tongs

Steps

1. Marinade & Prep

10min
30min
  • Massage dried sage, brown sugar, salt, garlic powder, and pepper into into tenderloins.
  • Let sit, covered, at room temperature 25 to 30 minutes.
  • *Meanwhile, prepare a gas or charcoal grill for a two-level fire (see "Set Up Your Grill: Two-Level Grilling," below), with one side at high heat and the other at medium (5 seconds with the hand test).

2. Sage Butter Sauce

20 min
  • In a small saucepan over medium-low heat, cook butter, olive oil, fresh sage, and garlic for about 10 minutes.
  • Stir in anchovy and remove from heat.
  • Let sit 10 minutes.
  • Strain butter and keep warm.

3. Seal Pork

20 min
10 min
  • Spray tenderloins generously with oil.
  • Arrange on a cleaned, oiled cooking grate over high heat.
  • Grill uncovered, turning occasionally, until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes total.
  • Move tenderloins to medium heat and grill, covered, turning occasionally, until 150Â° in centre and a touch of pink remaining.
  • Cook for about 1.5 minutes per cm thickness.
  • Remove from grill, tent with foil, and let pork rest 10 minutes before carving.

4. Serve

1 min
  • Cut into thin slices, garnish with sage sprigs, and serve with sage butter.
